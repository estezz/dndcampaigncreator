The Sunken Citadel of Xylos

Campaign Setting: Forgotten Realms

Party Size: 4-5

Character Levels: 5-7

Number of Sessions: 4

Number of Hours Per Session: 4

Location: The Sword Coast, near the ruins of a forgotten coastal city.

Setup

For centuries, the coastal city of Xylos was swallowed by the sea. Now, due to a series of unnatural storms, the ancient citadel has risen from the depths, its towers piercing the waves. Whispers of forgotten treasures and powerful, long-dormant creatures have reached the ears of adventurers. A local fisherman, Elara, recounts seeing strange lights emanating from the structure, and reports of unsettling tremors have sent the townsfolk into a panic. The party is contacted by the town council to investigate the disturbances and uncover the secrets of the Sunken Citadel. The players will be rewarded handsomely.

Plot Hooks

The Fisherman's Plea

Elara, a desperate fisherman, pleads for help. Her village is suffering from the strange occurrences and she's offering her family's heirloom (a simple, non-magical fishing rod) as payment. The party will be able to get other rewards upon successful completion.

The Scholarly Request

A scholar from Candlekeep, Arion, seeks brave adventurers. He requires a team to investigate the rumors and retrieve specific historical artifacts from the lost city of Xylos before they fall into the wrong hands. His resources offer to cover the cost to the party of supplies and other needs, as well as a large amount of gold upon completion.

Main Plot Steps

Investigating the Coastal Ruins

The party arrives at the coastal area, encountering signs of the unusual weather and the initial impact of the Citadel's rising. They can interact with the locals, gather information about the strange happenings, and prepare for a journey into the ruins. They will need to determine how to get to the location of the citadel. (Approx. 2 hours of gameplay)

Image Prompt: A group of adventurers standing on a windswept, rocky coast, looking out at the partially submerged ruins of an ancient city with ominous dark clouds overhead.

The Descent into Xylos

The party finds a way to approach the Citadel (boat, magical means, etc.) and begins their exploration of the partially submerged structures. They must navigate treacherous currents, fight off aquatic creatures, and find an entrance. The citadel's architecture is a mix of weathered stonework and eerie, intact sections. (Approx. 4 hours of gameplay)

Image Prompt: An underwater view of a partially submerged, ancient citadel. Sunlight filters through the water, illuminating weathered columns and crumbling arches. Schools of fish swim around the ruins.

The Guardian's Chamber

Deeper within the Citadel, the party encounters the Guardian of Xylos, a powerful aquatic creature (see stat block below) that protects the citadel's central chamber. This is the first major combat encounter. (Approx. 3 hours of gameplay)

Image Prompt: A colossal aquatic creature with bioluminescent features guarding a grand chamber within a sunken citadel. The chamber is lit by eerie blue light.

Unveiling the Citadel's Secret

After defeating the Guardian, the party enters the central chamber and discovers a powerful artifact, the Orb of Xylos. They learn the Citadel was not merely swallowed by the sea but was sealed away to contain a terrible magical force. They may face a final challenge, perhaps a powerful construct activated by the artifact. This culminates in a final combat encounter. (Approx. 3 hours of gameplay)

Image Prompt: A large, ornate chamber deep within a sunken citadel. In the center, a glowing orb pulsates with magical energy. Statues of ancient beings line the walls.
